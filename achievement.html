MY ACHIEVEMENT

One of the most meaningful achievements for me this year is overcoming my fear of heights. This success has given me the confidence to challenge myself and participate in activities that involve heights, which I used to avoid.

Looking back, the most significant achievement in my life so far is when I achieved excellent results in my studies, including straight A’s in my SPM and a CGPA of 4.0 in matriculation. In addition, I had the opportunity to represent my scout group and participate in the national Jota-Joti event (Jamboree on the Air - Jamboree on the Internet), which was a valuable experience.

Another meaningful achievement this year was when I successfully traveled abroad with my friends using our own money, without financial help from our families. This is something I’m really proud of, especially at the age of 19.

I also obtained my driving license this year without having to retake the test, which I consider a big accomplishment since it’s not easy to achieve. I’m very proud of this achievement.

Finally, my greatest accomplishment is getting the chance to further my studies at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM). This has been my dream since I was a child, and I am extremely proud to have made it a reality.
