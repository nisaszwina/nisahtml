MY HOMETOWN

My hometown is in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. Kelantan is known as the "Verandah of Mecca" because the state is renowned for its strong Islamic heritage, as well as its rich cultural traditions. Kota Bharu, being the capital of Kelantan, is a popular destination for tourists looking to experience the authentic culture of the state, which is deeply rooted in history and tradition.

The district is famous for its handicrafts, such as finely woven songket and traditional wau making, along with cultural performances like wayang kulit and dikir barat. In addition, traditional foods like laksam, nasi kerabu, and budu make Kota Bharu a paradise for food lovers.

Moreover, the area is also attractive because of its friendly community and their strong adherence to traditions and religion, making it a symbol of Kelantanese culture that is well preserved to this day.

Here are the top 5 tourist spots in my hometown Kota Bharu, Kelantan:

  1. Siti Khadijah Market
    2. •A must-visit traditional market for local goods, crafts, and food, where most of the vendors are women.
  2. Masjid Muhammadi
    3. • One of the oldest mosques in the city, known for its beautiful and historical Islamic architecture.
  3. Istana Jahar
    4. • A royal palace turned museum, offering a glimpse into Kelantan’s royal history and culture.
  4. Pantai Cahaya Bulan
    5. • A popular beach for relaxation and water activities, attracting both locals and tourists.
  5. Masjid Beijing (Sultan Ismail Petra Mosque)
    6. • A stunning mosque with unique Chinese architectural influences, located near the Thai-Malay border.
